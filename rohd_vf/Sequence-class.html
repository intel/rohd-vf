Sequence

Sequence<SequencerType extends Sequencer<SequenceItem>> class Null safety

A collection of operations to execute on a Sequencer.

Sequences are a mechanism that enabled modular reuse of a sequence of SequenceItems on a given type of Sequencer, parameterized by SequencerType.
